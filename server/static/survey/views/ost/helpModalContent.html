Understanding Survey Results

To dive deeper into Central Coast Monitoring Survey results:

  • Search on any term of interest to find monitoring projects containing that information.
  • Explore who is monitoring in the California Central Coast on the Explore Community page.
  • Explore what is being monitored and where monitoring is occurring on the Monitoring Activity page.
  • Filter monitoring projects by ecosystem features uniquely or in combination. Click in the filter field to activate filters.
  • Learn more about each monitoring project by clicking on the project in the table at the bottom of the page.
